Common Questions

How long does it take to build a system?+
Most systems are live within 2–6 weeks, depending on complexity. We build fast — no slow agency timelines. A basic WhatsApp + dashboard system can be running in under two weeks.
Do I need technical knowledge to use the system?+
No. We build systems that non-technical teams use daily. Your workers use WhatsApp as usual. Managers get a clean dashboard. No training required beyond a short onboarding call.
What does a System Audit involve?+
A 45-minute session where we map your current operational pain, identify the biggest bottlenecks, and show you what a custom system would look like. No commitment required — many clients say it's valuable on its own.
Can you maintain and improve the system after launch?+
Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and improvement retainers. Most clients keep us on a monthly basis to add features, fix issues and evolve the system as the business grows.
What industries do you work with?+
Property management, investment & finance, healthcare supply chain, and any business with real operational complexity. If you're managing people, inventory, clients or field work manually — we can build you a system.
